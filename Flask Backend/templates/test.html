How we do it ?

The Aadhaar Fraud Management System integrates cutting-edge AI and OCR technologies to streamline the process of verifying Aadhaar documents and preventing fraudulent activities. Our system works by automating data extraction, comparison, and scoring, providing accurate results quickly. The workflow involves users uploading ZIP and Excel files, the system classifying and detecting Aadhaar-related documents, extracting text using OCR, comparing data to the records, and generating a detailed report to help identify potential fraud cases.
